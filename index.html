See your entire life in one view.

Events Mode — Screens
Event Dashboard

Every moment you're looking forward to, in one place.

Your events dashboard shows all your upcoming (and past) countdowns in a gorgeous card layout. Each card shows the event name, its progress bar, and exactly how long to go — months, days, or hours.

  • Countdown CardsUpcoming events like graduations, trips, birthdays, and holidays each get their own vivid card with a bold time-remaining label.
  • Count Up CardsTrack ongoing streaks and milestones — like "Walking 5000 steps per day" — with a count-up timer that celebrates your consistency.
  • Progress BarsA visual progress bar on every card shows what percentage of the time has elapsed — perfect for seeing how close your event is at a glance.
Live Detail View

Days. Hours. Minutes. Seconds. Always counting.

Tap any event to open a full-screen detail view with a live countdown timer showing exact days, hours, minutes, and seconds — with your custom background image filling the screen behind it.

  • Real-Time PrecisionThe timer updates every second, so you feel the anticipation build in real time — never a vague "about 2 months".
  • Immersive BackgroundYour personal photo fills the entire screen. A Seoul cityscape for Korea Trip. A cap and gown for Graduation. Completely personal.
  • Share CountdownTap Share to send a beautiful countdown card to friends and family — build shared excitement for the moments ahead.
Smart Notifications

Never miss the moment you've been waiting for.

EverGlow sends you perfectly timed reminders so you're always ready. Whether it's a week out or the exact second your event arrives, you'll know.

  • 4-Stage AlertsNotifications fire automatically at 1 week, 1 day, 1 hour, and the exact moment — so you're never caught off guard.
